Our Story

Sully & Son Hydraulics opened its doors in 1977 by Jim (Sully) Sullivan and Son (Bob). Since that time, the company has expanded into a full-service hydraulic supply company with approximately 6500 sq. ft. of building space for sales, repairs, and fabrication. Jim (Sully) Sullivan began in the hydraulic field in 1957. As president of Sully & Son, his attitude toward customer service and hands on application has made the company well recognized in this field. Sully an Son Hydraulics is still a family owned and operated with a focus on customer service.
